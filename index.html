Who We Are

Sparky's Stuffies! exists to spread laughter and fun across Arizona State University through Stuffies of all kinds. College life is stressful; we get it! And we believe that a simple stuffed animal or plushy has the power to lighten a mood, draw a smile, and bring cheer to any atmosphere. That's why we are committed to bringing our Stuffies with us everywhere: classrooms, dining courts, events, you name it.
